About Us

Imperial Masters Ltd is a company that trades in fresh, safe and quality produce that is free from toxic pesticides. Imperial Fresh is a brand committed to providing fresh, safe, and quality food produce to our customers. We source our produce from organic and agro-ecological farmers who are conscious of how they produce their food to minimize the use of toxic chemicals and ensure that it is safe and nutritious.

Imperial Fresh seeks to improve agricultural production and market systems for farmers for the purpose of increasing incomes, attaining food security, increasing farm resilience to climate change, and natural capital maintenance. Our mission is to ensure safe, nutritious food is accessible to all at affordable prices.
